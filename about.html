ABOUT US

Lingua franca is the literary society of JSS, has actively organized literary and creative events and workshops for the benefit of the students.
Looking at the global professional scenario, the need of the hour is to acquire communication skills, and enhance these skills. We also conduct debates, extempore and group discussion and in addition literary activities like vocab-building. We at LINGUA FRANCA aim at helping new students ease into the college life, while also readying them for the years to come.

OUR EVENTS

Youth Parliament is a platform offered to the youngsters where they are given a mock parliament set up and exposure to the parliamentary type debate. Introducing national issues and acquainting youth to rules and regulations of India's parliament.
ALL ABOUT GRAFFITI
One of the most attractive events of college’s techno-cultural fest, ZEALICON. Here you get a chance to match your chemistry with someone you might have never seen in the college. Opposites do attract Might be true but not for this event. Here your luck decides where you get attracted. The event includes several stages where you get to know if you are in the correct pair or not. If not you might get another chance but not in the event again. First stage always follows the tradition of the society that is being influenced by the language. Your pair could the opposite of your word from the box or a meaning to your chit.
Turncoat is an English debate competition that enables the speaker to speak both in favour and against the motion within a given time frame testing one's knowledge,fluency and articulation and relevancy.
It is one of the most important and wonderful event of Zealicon that not only enhances one's speaking skills but also helps the person to showcase his/her knowledge and convincing ability. The participant will be given a topic and he or she has to speak on that particular topic for few minutes, both for and against the motion. In the end, the participant is supposed to conclude everything. It is an interesting event cherished by all.

A tale writing competition conducted digitally on our campus. An event in which we will take you through the basics of tale writing in which you’ll be given a specific word/theme to write on - after which you have the floor. After which the tales are curated promptly and the best ones will be published across our platforms. - Its sole purpose, is to take away all your excuses and bring you into a room just to write. - Its not as extensive as a workshop, but a few tips will be provided beforehand
